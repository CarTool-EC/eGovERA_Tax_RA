SV-Datasets Catalogue [Motivation] ()
NARRATIVE: The SV-Datasets Catalogue [Motivation] view extends Semantic view and provides a comprehensive framework for leveraging various elements related to data cataloguing and data quality. This view is primarily composed of four main elements: 'Business Agnostic Dataset Catalogue Solutions', 'Business Agnostic Business Information Base', 'Dataset Catalogue', and 'Dataset'. The 'Business Agnostic Dataset Catalogue Solutions' is a Grouping ABB that encompasses three Data Object SBBs: 'Eurostat Data Catalogue', 'Official Portal for European Data', and 'CELLAR'. Each of these data objects represents a unique data catalogue solution. The 'Eurostat Data Catalogue' is a comprehensive repository of statistical data maintained by EUROSTAT, the statistical office of the European Union (EU). The 'Official Portal for European Data' serves as a central point of access to European open data from various international, national, regional, local, and geodata portals. 'CELLAR' is the common data repository of the Publications Office of the European Union, storing digital publications and metadata for use by humans and machines. The 'Business Agnostic Business Information Base' is a Diagram Model-group ABB that includes two Business Object ABBs: 'Availability' and 'Data Quality'. These objects represent critical aspects of business information, with 'Availability' referring to the accessibility of the data and 'Data Quality' referring to the integrity and reliability of the data. The 'Dataset Catalogue' is a Data Object ABB that represents an inventory of datasets indexed in a systematic manner. This object is crucial for organizing and managing the datasets within the catalogue. The 'Dataset' is another Data Object ABB that represents a collection of related data organized and presented in a structured format. This object is fundamental to the concept of data cataloguing as it represents the actual data that is being catalogued. The relationships among these elements are defined by their hierarchical structure within the JSON. The 'Business Agnostic Dataset Catalogue Solutions' and 'Business Agnostic Business Information Base' are overarching elements that encompass more specific data objects and business objects, respectively. The 'Dataset Catalogue' and 'Dataset' are standalone elements that represent key aspects of the data cataloguing process. The motivation behind these elements and their relationships is to provide a comprehensive and structured approach to data cataloguing, ensuring data quality and availability.
