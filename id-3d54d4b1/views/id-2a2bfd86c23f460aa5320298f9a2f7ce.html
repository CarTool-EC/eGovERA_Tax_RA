TVA-Tax Management Enablers [Motivation] ()
NARRATIVE: The TVA-Tax Management Enablers [Motivation] view extends Technical view - application and provides a comprehensive framework for leveraging various application services and solutions for efficient tax management. The view is divided into two main groupings: Tax Management Enablers and Tax Management Solutions. The Tax Management Enablers grouping consists of several Application Service ABBs that facilitate various aspects of tax management. These include the Tax Calendar Management Service, which aids in scheduling and tracking tax-related activities, and the Tax Coding System Management, which helps in managing the tax coding system. The Online Restricted Access To National Tax Database System provides secure access to the national tax database, while the Tax Virtual Assistant Service offers virtual assistance for tax-related queries. The Tax Customer Messaging Solution facilitates communication with taxpayers, and the Operational Tax Application supports the operational aspects of tax management. The Profit Shifting Discovery and Full Base Discovery services aid in discovering profit shifting and full base respectively. Lastly, the Tax Analytics Service provides analytics related to tax. The Tax Management Solutions grouping comprises several Application Service SBBs that offer comprehensive solutions for tax management. These include SEED, which is a specific application service, and the Transaction Network Analysis service, which analyses transaction networks. The Surveillance System (import Declaration And ECommerce) monitors import declarations and eCommerce, while SPEED 2 (tax And Customs Administrations) facilitates tax and customs administrations. The Tax Digital Information Gateway provides a digital gateway for tax information, and the Customs Decisions System aids in making customs decisions. The Customs Information System provides information related to customs, and OpenPeppol is a specific application service. The Tax Information Portal Service offers a portal for tax information, EMCS is a specific application service, and the Import Control System 2 (ICS 2) manages the import control system. Each of these elements within the groupings play a crucial role in enabling efficient tax management, and their interrelations and motivations are driven by the overarching goal of improving tax management processes and systems.
