Tax Management Enablers ()
Definition: Tax Management Enablers ABB is a Grouping that covers a range of application services designed to facilitate and streamline various aspects of tax management. These services include tax calendar management, tax coding system management, restricted access to national tax databases, tax virtual assistant services, tax customer messaging solutions, operational tax applications, profit shifting discovery, full base discovery, and tax analytics services. Source: EIRA Team Research Additional information: The Tax Management Enablers ABB is a comprehensive suite of services that cater to different aspects of tax management. These services are designed to simplify and enhance the process of managing taxes, from keeping track of tax deadlines with the tax calendar management service, to managing tax coding systems, to providing restricted access to national tax databases. The suite also includes a tax virtual assistant service for automated assistance, a tax customer messaging solution for effective communication, operational tax applications for day-to-day tax operations, and services for profit shifting discovery and full base discovery. Additionally, the suite offers a tax analytics service for data-driven insights into tax management. Example: For instance, a business could use the tax calendar management service to keep track of important tax deadlines and avoid penalties. The tax coding system management service could be used to manage and update the business's tax codes as needed. The online restricted access to national tax database system could provide the business with important tax information, while the tax virtual assistant service could provide automated assistance with tax-related queries. The tax customer messaging solution could be used to communicate effectively with customers about tax-related matters. The operational tax application could be used for day-to-day tax operations, while the profit shifting discovery and full base discovery services could be used to discover potential areas of profit shifting and to conduct a full base discovery. Finally, the tax analytics service could provide the business with data-driven insights into their tax management.
