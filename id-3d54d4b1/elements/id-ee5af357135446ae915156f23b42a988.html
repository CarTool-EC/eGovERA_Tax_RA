Tax Management Solutions ()
Definition: Tax Management Solutions ABB is a grouping that covers a range of application services designed to facilitate various aspects of tax management. These services include SEED, Transaction Network Analysis, Surveillance System (import Declaration And ECommerce), SPEED 2 (tax And Customs Administrations), Tax Digital Information Gateway, Customs Decisions System, Customs Information System, OpenPeppol, Tax Information Portal Service, EMCS, and Import Control System 2 (ICS 2). Source: EIRA Team Research Additional information: The elements within the Tax Management Solutions ABB are all application services aimed at streamlining and enhancing various aspects of tax management. These include services for transaction network analysis, import declaration and eCommerce surveillance, tax and customs administration, digital information gateways for tax, customs decision systems, customs information systems, and more. Each service is designed to facilitate a specific aspect of tax management, providing comprehensive solutions for tax and customs administrations. Example: For instance, the 'SEED Application Service' might be used by tax authorities to verify the status of economic operators, while the 'Transaction Network Analysis Application Service' could be used to analyze transaction networks for potential tax fraud. The 'Surveillance System (import Declaration And ECommerce) Application Service' could be used to monitor import declarations and eCommerce transactions for compliance with tax laws, and the 'SPEED 2 (tax And Customs Administrations) Application Service' could be used to expedite tax and customs administrations.
