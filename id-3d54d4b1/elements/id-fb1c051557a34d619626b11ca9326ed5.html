Customs information system ()
Description: The Customs Information System Supervision Coordination Group (CIS SCG) is a body established by Regulation (EC) No 766/2008 to ensure coordinated supervision in the area of personal data protection of the CIS information system. The CIS SCG is composed of representatives from the National Supervisory Authorities of the Member States responsible for data protection and the European Data Protection Supervisor. Additional information: The Customs Information System (CIS) is a system designed to enhance customs cooperation and mutual assistance between Member States and to protect the financial interests of the European Union. The CIS SCG is responsible for ensuring that the CIS operates within the boundaries of data protection laws, safeguarding the personal data of individuals. The group is composed of representatives from each Member State's National Supervisory Authorities, which are responsible for data protection, as well as the European Data Protection Supervisor. Example: An example of the application of the Customs Information System is when customs authorities in one Member State input data about a suspicious shipment into the CIS. This information can then be accessed by customs authorities in other Member States, allowing for coordinated action. The CIS SCG would ensure that any personal data related to this shipment is handled in accordance with data protection laws.
