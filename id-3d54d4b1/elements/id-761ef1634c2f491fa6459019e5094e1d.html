dct:titleRegulation (EU) 2018/1724 establishing a single digital gateway to provide access to information, to procedures and to assistance and problem-solving services
dct:descriptionRegulation (EU) 2018/1724 establishes a single digital gateway that aims to provide easy access to information, procedures, and assistance and problem-solving services for citizens and businesses within the European Union. It aims to simplify and streamline the process of accessing and using public services across EU member states by offering a centralized digital platform.
dct:publisherEUR-Lex
dct:sourcehttps://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/TXT/?uri=CELEX%3A32018R1724
skos:exampleAn EU citizen residing in France wants to start a business in Germany. Instead of navigating through multiple websites and bureaucratic processes, they can use the single digital gateway to find information on the necessary procedures, requirements, and assistance available. They can also access problem-solving services in case they encounter any issues during the process. This simplifies the cross-border business establishment and ensures a smoother experience for the citizen.
eira:concepteira:SolutionBuildingBlock
skos:noteThe regulation aims to enhance the digital experience of citizens and businesses by providing a single entry point for accessing information and services across the EU. It promotes transparency, efficiency, and accessibility of public services, ultimately contributing to the digital transformation of the EU.
