Tax Digital Information Gateway ()
Description: The Tax Digital Information Gateway is a digital platform that facilitates tax administration by providing access to tax-related information owned by other Member States or different public administrations. Additional information: The Tax Digital Information Gateway is a crucial tool in the modernization of tax administration. It enables seamless sharing and access to tax-related information across different jurisdictions, thereby enhancing transparency, efficiency, and cooperation among tax authorities. The gateway can provide information on a variety of tax-related matters, including income tax, corporate tax, value-added tax (VAT), and other forms of direct and indirect taxation. It is particularly useful in the context of cross-border transactions and operations, where tax-related information from different Member States may be required. Example: For instance, if a business entity operates in multiple European Union Member States, the Tax Digital Information Gateway can provide the respective tax authorities with access to the necessary tax-related information from each jurisdiction. This can facilitate the process of tax assessment and collection, ensuring compliance with the tax laws of each Member State. Similarly, in the case of an individual taxpayer with income sources in different Member States, the gateway can provide the relevant tax authorities with access to the necessary income information, thereby facilitating the process of income tax assessment and collection.
