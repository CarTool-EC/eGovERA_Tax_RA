SPEED 2 (tax and customs administrations) ()
Description: SPEED 2 represents the use or consumption of features provided by the SPEED enabling a single point of entry for tax and customs administration for consuming digital public services and exchange data. Additional information: SPEED 2 is a system that is designed to streamline the process of tax and customs administration. It provides a single point of entry for these administrations to consume digital public services and exchange data. This not only simplifies the process but also makes it more efficient. The system is designed to handle a variety of tasks related to tax and customs administration, including the collection and management of tax data, the processing of customs declarations, and the exchange of information with other government agencies and entities. It is a key tool in the digital transformation of public services, enabling a more seamless and efficient administration of tax and customs. Example: An example of SPEED 2 in action could be a customs agency using the system to process import and export declarations. The agency can use SPEED 2 to access the necessary digital services, input the relevant data, and then exchange this data with other relevant agencies. Similarly, a tax administration could use SPEED 2 to manage tax data, using the system to access digital services for data collection and management, and then exchanging this data with other relevant entities.
