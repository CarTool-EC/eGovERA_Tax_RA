Customs Decisions System ()
Description: The Customs Decisions System (CDS) is a digital platform that facilitates the submission of all new applications for customs decisions or authorisations electronically. It is designed to streamline and simplify the process of applying for customs decisions, making it more efficient and user-friendly. Additional information: The Customs Decisions System (CDS) is a significant part of the digital transformation in customs procedures. It is designed to provide a more efficient, streamlined process for businesses and individuals applying for customs decisions or authorisations. The system allows users to submit applications electronically, reducing the need for paper-based applications and speeding up the decision-making process. It also provides a centralised location for all applications, making it easier for customs authorities to manage and process applications. Example: For instance, a business importing goods from a non-EU country can use the CDS to apply for a customs decision on the classification of their goods. They would submit their application electronically through the system, providing all necessary information and documentation. The customs authorities would then review the application and make a decision, which would be communicated to the business through the system.
