eProcurement Data Model Solutions ()
Definition: eProcurement Data Model Solutions ABB is a Grouping that covers the elements related to the data models used in electronic procurement (eProcurement) processes. These elements are designed to standardize and streamline the procurement processes, particularly in the public sector within the European Union (EU). The primary element in this grouping is the European Single Procurement Document (ESPD), a standardized form used in public procurement processes within the EU. Source: EIRA Team Research Additional information: The eProcurement Data Model Solutions ABB focuses on the ESPD-EDM element. The European Single Procurement Document (ESPD) is a structured document that contains essential information about a supplier or contractor, including company identification, financial standing, technical and professional abilities, and any legal grounds for exclusion. The ESPD serves as a preliminary self-declaration by suppliers, allowing them to demonstrate their eligibility and suitability to participate in public procurement procedures across EU member states. As a data model, it helps to standardize and streamline the procurement processes, making them more efficient and transparent. Example: An example of the application of the eProcurement Data Model Solutions ABB is in public procurement processes within the EU. When a public sector organization is seeking to procure goods or services, suppliers or contractors can use the ESPD to provide essential information about their company and demonstrate their eligibility and suitability to participate in the procurement process. The standardized format of the ESPD makes it easier for the procuring organization to compare and evaluate the suppliers or contractors.
